Deliverables

This assignment uses two files: a Figma FigJam board for the sitemap and a Figma Design file for the content inventory

  1. Post a link to your Figma FigJam (use the blue share button and copy link button).
    • This will be a different file/link than your other p2: Skeuomorphic Echoes assignments.
  2. Post a link to your Figma Design file containing your sitemap screenshot and content inventory frames.
    • This will be the same file/link as your other p2: Skeuomorphic Echoes assignments.
  3. Post one screenshot showing your sitemap and content inventory together (zoom out to capture it all).

 

Requirements

  1. Sitemap in Figma FigJam
    • List and then map the relationship between all pages/screens.
      Items to include in your sitemap are:
      • Home page/start screen (begin here)
      • About/overview/sources page
      • List page
      • Interior pages
      • Modals
        • popups
        • messages
        • menus
        • nav drawers
        • dropdowns
      • Any other piece of content the user can navigate to
  2. Content Inventory in Figma Design on Research page
    • Make a content inventory for each major page.
    • Home page example:
      • Title: text
      • Logo: image/icon
      • Nav: list of links
      • Hero image: image, video
      • Intro: text
      • Socials: list of links
    • About page example:
      • Title: text
      • Subheading: text
      • Nav: list of links
      • Photo: portrait
      • Bio: text
      • Contact info: text/email
      • Project overview: text
      • Sources
        • Works cited: text + URL
        • Image credits: text + URL
        • Figma Plugins: text + URL
    • Interior page example:
      • Title: text
      • Subheading: text
      • Nav: list of links, previous/next buttons, close button
      • 5-10 project-specific pieces of information
