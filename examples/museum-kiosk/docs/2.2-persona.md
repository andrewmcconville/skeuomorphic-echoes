## Bio

Maya just finished her MFA in Art & Design + Technology and is decompressing by visiting museums and design exhibitions—part research, part therapy. She's obsessed with how interface conventions become "invisible culture," and she loves exhibits that treat everyday UI details like artifacts worth studying.

## Goals *(optional)*

- Discover the history behind familiar interface icons, sounds, and language.
- Save/collect references (artifacts, favorites, achievements) to revisit later.
