Deliverables

  1. Post one photo of your paper wireframes (photograph all of your sketches laid out together).
  2. Post a link to your Figma file. Place your wireframe photos on the Research page.
    • This will be the same file/link as your other p2: Skeuomorphic Echoes assignments.

 

Requirements

  1. Sketch your screen layouts on paper
    • Use your Sitemaps and Content Inventories from p2.3: Sitemap & Content Inventory as your guide.
    • Sketch one screen per piece of paper (keep it fast and rough, that's the point).
    • Include the key content and UI elements from your Content Inventory in each frame.
  2. Check your layouts against your Happy Paths
    • Use your two Happy Paths from p2.4: Happy Path & User Task to walk through your sketches step by step.
    • Ask yourself: does the layout support the user's flow? Is anything missing or out of order?
    • Make adjustments to your sketches as needed.
  3. In-class partner review
    • Pair up with a partner.
    • Read them your two User Tasks and ask them to complete the tasks using only your paper wireframes.
    • Observe, don't guide them. Note where they hesitate or get confused.
