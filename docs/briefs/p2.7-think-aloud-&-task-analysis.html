Deliverables

  1. Post a link to your Figma file (use the blue share button and copy link button).
    • This will be the same file/link as your other p2: Skeuomorphic Echoes assignments.
  2. Post one screenshot from Figma showing your Task Analysis (zoom out to capture the entire screen).

 

Instructions

  1. Pair up in groups of two
  2. Open your Figma wireframe from p2.6: Lo-fi Wireframe in Figma
  3. Give your partner your user task from p2.4: Happy Path & User Task
    • Task Analysis: Designer: record and take notes on the actions your participant performs (clicks, scrolls, gets stuck, etc.)
    • Think-Aloud: Participant: speak your thoughts, what do you see, what are you thinking, what looks good, what's confusing
  4. Switch roles
  5. Repeat steps 3–5 with two more partners

 

Requirements

  1. Document the following in your Figma's Research page
    1. Did your participant follow the happy path exactly as intended?
    2. How did your participant deviate from or question the happy path?
    3. What did they find the most intuitive and should be reused in the design?
    4. What did they find the least intuitive and could use a redesign?
  2. Make at least two comments (Figma comments, hotkey: C) on your wireframe where the experience:
    1. went well
    2. needs improvement
