## User Task 1 — Explore a Specimen and Collect It

1. **Context:** You're standing in front of a museum kiosk running *Before the Prompt: The Rise and Fall of Digital Icons*. The screen shows a top-down floor plan with four highlighted rooms.
2. **Scenario:** You're curious about how modern word processors inherited their design from older technologies, and you want to save an interesting artifact to your personal collection.
3. **Task:** Starting from the floor plan, navigate to the WYSIWYG room, open the Google Docs evolutionary stage, and collect it.

---

## User Task 2 — Review Collection Progress and Navigate to an Unvisited Stage

1. **Context:** You've already been using the *Before the Prompt* kiosk for a few minutes and have collected some specimens. The screen shows the floor plan home screen.
2. **Scenario:** You want to check which specimens you've already collected in the Camera Shutter room and find one you haven't visited yet.
3. **Task:** Using the Visited Rooms screen, find the Camera Shutter room, check your progress, then navigate to the Box Camera stage you haven't seen yet.
