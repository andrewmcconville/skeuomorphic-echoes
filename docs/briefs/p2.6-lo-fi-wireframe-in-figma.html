Requirements

  1. Make a new Figma "Page" titled Lo-fi Wireframe.
  2. Choose one of your two Happy Paths from p2.4: Happy Path & User Task.
    • For each screen/page/overlay in the Happy Path workflow:
      • Include all written text content the user would see on that screen (navigation, headings, body copy, buttons, links, labels). For large (more than two sentences) areas of body copy, you may use lorem ipsum.
      • Include placeholders for icons, images, videos, graphics. Can be gray boxes.
  3. Wire all your frames and make them interactive.
    • Stub out the rest of your screens with at least a page title.
    • Create a working clickable prototype.
    • Add working navigation to all pages.
    • Add scrolling, overlays, hovers, and other functionality.

 

You can come back at the end and brand it if you have time, but don't get caught up in creating and including graphical assets.
