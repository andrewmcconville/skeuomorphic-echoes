## User Task 1 — Explore an Artifact and Collect It

1. **Context:** You're standing in front of a museum kiosk running *Before the Prompt: The Rise and Fall of Digital Icons*. The screen shows a top-down floor plan with four highlighted rooms.
2. **Scenario:** You're curious about how modern word processors inherited their design from older technologies, and you want to save an interesting artifact to your personal collection.
3. **Task:** Starting from the floor plan, navigate to the WYSIWYG room, open the Google Docs artifact, and collect it.

---

## User Task 2 — Review Artifact Collection and Navigate to an Uncollected Artifact

1. **Context:** You've already been using the *Before the Prompt* kiosk for a few minutes and have collected some artifacts. The screen shows the floor plan home screen.
2. **Scenario:** You want to check which artifacts you've already collected in the Camera Shutter room and find one you haven't collected yet.
3. **Task:** Using the Artifact Collection screen, find the Camera Shutter group, check your progress, then navigate to the Box Camera artifact you haven't collected yet.
